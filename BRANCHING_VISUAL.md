## 🏆 Progression Summary

| Proficiency | Lesson Path | Quiz Threshold | Game Difficulty | XP Multiplier |
|------------|-------------|----------------|-----------------|---------------|
| **Beginner** | Main + Interventions | Must score ≥ 60% | Easy (more time, hints) | 1.0x |
| **Intermediate** | Main path only | Score ≥ 60% to proceed | Standard | 1.2x |
| **Advanced** | Main + Enrichment | Score ≥ 85% for extras | Hard (timed, complex) | 1.5x |
