Section 01

Getting Started

Welcome to LiteRise — an adaptive reading and literacy app designed to help students learn at their own pace. Follow the steps below to get the app up and running on your Android phone.

💡 What you'll need: An Android smartphone and a working internet connection to create your account and load lessons.
  1. 1

    Install the App

    Download LiteRise from the Google Play Store or install the APK file shared by your teacher/school.

  2. 2

    Open the App

    Tap the LiteRise icon on your home screen. The app will open with a short splash screen while it loads.

  3. 3

    Choose to Login or Register

    If you're new, tap Register to create a free account. If you already have one, tap Login.

Section 03

Logging In

Already have an account? Logging in only takes a few seconds.

  1. 1

    Enter your Email

    Type the email address you used when signing up.

  2. 2

    Enter your Password

    Tap the eye icon 👁 to show or hide your password as you type.

  3. 3

    Tap "Login"

    The app will check your info and bring you straight to your dashboard.

🔓 Forgot your password?

Tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen. Enter your email and you'll receive steps to reset it. Check your inbox (and spam folder!) after a minute.

Section 05

Placement Test

Before your first lesson, LiteRise gives you a short reading check. This helps the app understand your current level so it can give you the right lessons — not too easy, not too hard.

📋 About the Test

The placement test has 25 questions across 5 reading skill areas. It adapts as you go — meaning if you answer correctly, the next question may be slightly harder, and vice versa. There's no passing or failing. Just answer honestly!

5 Skill Areas Covered

#Skill AreaWhat It Checks
1📗 Phonics & Word StudyLetter sounds, blends, word patterns
2📙 Vocabulary & Word KnowledgeWord meanings, context clues
3📘 Grammar AwarenessSentence structure, parts of speech
4📕 Comprehending TextReading passages, main idea, details
5📓 Creating & ComposingWriting ideas, sentence completion

Section 07

Taking a Lesson

Every lesson in LiteRise follows a simple 3-phase process: Learn → Practice → Results. Here's how it works step by step.

📚

Phase 1 — Learn

Read through the lesson content at your own pace

↓
✏️

Phase 2 — Practice Quiz

Answer 5–10 questions to test what you've learned

↓
🏆

Phase 3 — Results

See your score, earn XP, and unlock the next lesson

Phase 1 — Learn

The lesson page shows you reading content, examples, vocabulary, and grammar rules. Read through everything, then tap "Complete Lesson" at the bottom to move on.

Phase 2 — Practice Quiz

After the lesson, a short quiz will appear. Select the best answer for each question and tap "Submit" when done. There's a progress bar at the top so you always know how many questions are left.

📸 Screenshot — Quiz Screen

Phase 3 — Quiz Results & What Happens Next

Based on your score, LiteRise will decide the best next step for you automatically.

Score 80–89%

✓ Ready to Proceed

Great job! The next lesson is now unlocked. Tap "Continue Adventure" to keep going.

Score 90%+ (Advanced)

🏆 Enrichment Available

Outstanding! You can take on an optional challenge lesson for extra practice and XP.

Score 70–79% (Beginner)

💡 Extra Practice Available

Good effort! An optional supplemental lesson is unlocked to help reinforce the topic.

Score Below 70%

⚠ More Practice Needed

No worries! A shorter remedial lesson is unlocked first. Complete it before moving on.

Section 09

Badges & Achievements

Badges are your trophies inside LiteRise! You earn them by hitting milestones in your learning journey. Collect them all!

How to Earn Badges

  • Complete your first lesson
  • Finish an entire module
  • Keep a daily streak for several days in a row
  • Score 90% or higher on a quiz
  • Complete the post-assessment

🎉 You Earned a Badge!

When you unlock a new badge, a celebration pop-up appears automatically — even in the middle of a lesson! Tap anywhere to close it and continue.

Section 10

Progress & Reports

Want to see how far you've come? The Progress tab shows all your learning stats in one place.

What's in the Progress Tab

📅 Daily Activity

A calendar or chart showing which days you studied and how active you were.

⭐ Total XP

Your all-time experience points accumulated from lessons, quizzes, and games.

📈 Skills Breakdown

A detailed view showing your strength level in each of the 5 reading skill areas.

Pre vs Post Comparison Report

After you complete enough lessons, you can take a final check-up test. The Comparison Report then shows you side-by-side how much you improved across every skill area since you first started.

Section 12

Certificates

LiteRise rewards your hard work with a digital certificate when you complete the program. This is a great way to show your achievement!

How to Get Your Certificate

  1. 1

    Complete the Post-Assessment

    After finishing your lessons, you'll be prompted to take a final check-up test.

  2. 2

    View Your Results

    The results screen will show your improvement compared to when you first started.

  3. 3

    Download or Share Your Certificate

    Tap the "Get Certificate" button on the results screen to save it to your phone or share it directly.
